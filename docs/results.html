How to read your results

  • Everything is on a scale of 0-10 except for the color aspect
  • The X Axis is Economical Insights with 0 being a command market economy and 10 being a Laissez Faire economy
  • The Y Axis is Government Policies with 0 being libertarian and 10 begin authoritarian
  • The Z Axis is Societal Norms with 0 being traditional and 10 being progressive
  • The color aspect goes from red to pink and follows the pattern of the rainbow (with the addition of pink) with red being closest to a group-oriented person and pink being an individualistic person
